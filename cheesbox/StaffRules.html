Staff rules for every member of the staff team

1.

Do not abuse your power as a staff member. This includes using commands or privileges to gain an advantage over players or to bully them.

2.

Respect players and their opinions. Do not insult, discriminate, or harass players based on their race, gender, sexuality, religion, or any other characteristic.

3.

Handle all reports and complaints from players in a timely and professional manner. Make sure to gather all necessary evidence before taking action.

4.

Do not use your staff position to promote personal projects or gain personal benefits.

5.

Be active on the server and engage with players. Help new players get started, and answer questions from players.

6.

Do not give special treatment to friends or players you know outside of the server.

7.

Report any bugs or glitches to higher staff members as soon as possible.

8.

Keep the server and its community a safe and enjoyable place for all players.

9.

Follow the server's specific guidelines and rules, and communicate with higher staff members if you have any questions or concerns.

10.

Continuously educate yourself about the game and its updates, and be willing to learn new things to improve your performance as a staff member.

11.

Maintain a positive attitude and lead by example for other staff members and players.

12.

Do not share any personal information about players or other staff members without their permission.

13.

Be accountable for your actions and decisions as a staff member, and take responsibility for any mistakes or issues that arise.

Overall, the main goal for staff members should be to create a welcoming and safe environment for players to enjoy the game.
