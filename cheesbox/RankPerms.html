Perks / Permissions of every Rank with description

Owner

The owner is the highest rank on the server and has complete control over all aspects of the server, including management, configuration, and decision-making. They are responsible for making sure the server is running smoothly and resolving any issues that arise.

<

Co Owner

The co-owner works closely with the owner and is responsible for assisting with server management, making decisions, and ensuring the server is running smoothly. They may have the authority to make decisions and perform actions on behalf of the owner.

Staff Manager

The staff manager is responsible for managing the staff team and ensuring that they are performing their duties effectively. They may be responsible for training new staff members, monitoring staff performance, and handling disciplinary action if necessary..

Head Developer

The head developer is responsible for managing and leading the development team, overseeing the creation and maintenance of plugins.

Head Admin

The head admin is responsible for managing and leading the administrative team, overseeing the enforcement of server rules and policies, and ensuring that the server is running smoothly.

Head Moderator

The head moderator is responsible for managing and leading the moderation team, overseeing the enforcement of server rules and policies, and ensuring that the server is a safe and enjoyable environment for all players.

Head Helper

The head helper is responsible for managing and leading the helper team, assisting players with questions and concerns, and ensuring that the server is a welcoming and helpful environment.

Senior Developer

The senior developer works closely with the head developer and is responsible for developing and maintaining plugins, fixing bugs, and implementing new features on the server.

Senior Admin

The senior admin works closely with the head admin and is responsible for enforcing server rules and policies, handling player complaints, and ensuring that the server is running smoothly.

Developer

The developer is responsible for creating and maintaining plugins, fixing bugs, and implementing new features on the server.

Admin

The admin is responsible for enforcing server rules and policies, handling player complaints, and ensuring that the server is running smoothly.

Junior Developer

The junior developer is a newer member of the development team who is responsible for learning from and working closely with the senior developers to develop and maintain plugins and implement new features.

Junior Admin

A staff member who has some administrative permissions, such as being able to ban users and view server logs. They are responsible for helping to manage the server and ensure that everything runs smoothly.

Senior Moderator

A staff member who has some moderation permissions, such as being able to kick users and mute chat. They are responsible for helping to moderate the server and enforce its rules.

Senior Helper

A staff member who has some helper permissions, such as being able to answer player questions and offer assistance. They are responsible for helping players and ensuring that they have a positive experience on the server.

Moderator

A staff member who has basic moderation permissions, such as being able to mute or kick players. They are responsible for enforcing the server's rules and helping to maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for all players.

Helper

A staff member who is responsible for assisting players with any questions or issues they may have. They are often the first point of contact for new players and can help them get started on the server.

Junior Moderator

A staff member who is still learning the ropes of moderation. They have limited permissions, but are still responsible for enforcing the server's rules and helping to maintain a safe environment.

Junior Helper

A staff member who is still learning the ropes of assisting players. They have limited permissions, but are still responsible for helping players and ensuring that they have a positive experience on the server.

Tester

A staff member who is responsible for testing new features and reporting any bugs or issues they encounter. They help ensure that the server runs smoothly and that new features are working as intended.

Trainee

A new staff member who is undergoing training and learning the responsibilities of their rank. They have limited permissions, but are still responsible for upholding the server's rules and helping players as needed.

The ranks are listed here from their position in the staff team
